About Us

Established in 2020 as a Non-Profit Company, the Bumblebee Foundation is dedicated to serving as a bridge for both local and international sponsors who wish to make a significant impact in South Africa. Our primary focus is on forming partnerships with offshore sponsors, leveraging their resources to support the initiatives of other non-profit organizations.

Our Mission

At the Bumblebee Foundation, we are committed to fostering partnerships and mobilizing resources to support initiatives of non-profit organizations in South Africa. We aim to connect offshore sponsors with local projects, amplifying their impact and empowering communities to tackle pressing social and environmental challenges.

Success Stories

Empowering Youth Through Education

Through a partnership with a UK-based sponsor, Bumblebee Foundation has helped fund educational programs for underprivileged youth in South Africa. Over 200 students have benefited from scholarships and mentorship programs, helping them achieve their dreams.

Supporting Healthcare Initiatives

In collaboration with international healthcare foundations, Bumblebee Foundation has facilitated the delivery of critical medical supplies and infrastructure to rural communities, improving healthcare access for over 5000 individuals.

Environmental Conservation Efforts

Bumblebee’s strategic alignment with global environmental groups has enabled large-scale reforestation projects, restoring biodiversity and creating job opportunities in local communities across South Africa.
